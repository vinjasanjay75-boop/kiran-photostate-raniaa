📢 Recruitment Overview

The Delhi Subordinate Services Selection Board (DSSSB) conducts recruitment for various Group B and Group C posts under the Government of NCT of Delhi. These posts include Junior Assistant, TGT, PGT, Clerk, LDC, Assistant Teacher, and many more.

🎓 Educational Qualification

  • 10th / 12th / Graduate / Post Graduate depending on the post.
  • Professional qualifications may be required for specific posts (e.g., B.Ed for teaching posts).
  • Typing or computer knowledge may be required for clerical positions.

📊 Vacancy Details

The exact number of vacancies will be announced in the official notification. Vacancies are released for multiple departments under the Delhi Government.

📝 Selection Process

  • Tier-I Written Examination (Computer-Based Test)
  • Tier-II Examination (For specific posts)
  • Skill Test / Typing Test (if applicable)
  • Document Verification

💼 Salary Details

Selected candidates will receive salaries as per the 7th Pay Commission, generally ranging from ₹19,900 to ₹1,12,400 depending on the post and pay level.
