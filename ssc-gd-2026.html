📢 Recruitment Overview

The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) conducts the GD Constable Examination to recruit candidates for various paramilitary forces such as BSF, CISF, CRPF, SSB, ITBP, Assam Rifles, and Sepoy in NCB. This is one of the most popular government job opportunities for 10th pass candidates.
