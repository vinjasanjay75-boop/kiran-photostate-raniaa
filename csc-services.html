💳 PAN Card (Naya/Correction)

📂 Jaruri Documents:

Aadhaar Card (Mobile Number Link hona chahiye), 2 Passport Photos.

📝 Kaise Apply Karein:

1. NSDL Portal par 'Online PAN Application' select karein.
2. Form 49A bharein aur ₹107 ki fees pay karein.
3. Aadhaar OTP se authentication karein.
4. 10-15 din mein PAN Card ghar aa jayega.
NSDL Apply Link
🆔 Voter ID Card (NVSP)

📂 Jaruri Documents:

Aadhaar Card, 10th Marksheet, 1 Color Photo.

📝 Kaise Apply Karein:

1. Voters Service Portal par Account banayein.
2. Naye Voter ke liye **'Form 6'** bharein.
3. Correction ke liye **'Form 8'** bharein.
4. BLO verification ke baad card ban jayega.
Official Voter Portal
🏥 Ayushman Bharat Card

📂 Jaruri Documents:

Family ID (PPP), Aadhaar Card.

📝 Kaise Check Karein:

1. Beneficiary Portal par login karein.
2. Family ID number daalkar members ki list dekhein.
3. Jis member ka card nahi bana, uska Aadhaar KYC karein.
4. Card approve hone par wahin se download karein.
