📝 Kaise Apply Karein? (Step-by-Step Guide)

Step 1: Document Taiyar Karein

Sabse pehle zaruri documents scan karke rakhlein (Aadhaar, Photo, Signature aur Marksheet). File ka size 200kb se kam rakhein.

Step 2: Official Link Par Jayein

Upar diye gaye list mein se jo service aapko chahiye uske "Link" par click karein. Ye aapko official govt website par le jayega.

Step 3: Registration / Login

Website khulne ke baad 'New Registration' par click karein aur apna Mobile Number daalkar OTP se verify karein.

Step 4: Form Bharna Start Karein

Apni saari detail (Naam, Pita ka naam, DOB) documents ke hisaab se dhayan se bharein. Koi bhi galati na karein.

Step 5: Fees Payment

Agar service ki koi govt fee hai, toh use UPI, Net Banking ya ATM card se pay karein. Payment hone ke baad page ko refresh na karein.

Step 6: Final Printout

Form submit hone ke baad "Acknowledgement Receipt" download karein. Aap hamari shop **Kiran Photostate Rania** par aakar iska print nikalwa sakte hain.
