📢 Recruitment Overview

Haryana Staff Selection Commission (HSSC) conducts recruitment for various Group D posts in different departments of the Haryana Government. These posts may include Peon, Chowkidar, Helper, Mali, Sweeper, and other support staff.

🎓 Educational Qualification

  • Passed 10th (Matriculation) from a recognized board.
  • Knowledge of Hindi/Sanskrit up to Matric level.
  • Additional qualifications may be required for specific posts.

📝 Selection Process

  • Common Eligibility Test (CET) Score
  • Socio-Economic Criteria & Experience
  • Document Verification
  • Medical Examination

💼 Salary Details

Selected candidates will receive a salary under the 7th Pay Commission, generally ranging from ₹16,900 to ₹53,500 (Pay Level-1) along with applicable allowances.
