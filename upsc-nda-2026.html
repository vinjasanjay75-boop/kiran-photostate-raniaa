📢 Recruitment Overview

The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) conducts the National Defence Academy (NDA) and Naval Academy (NA) Examination twice a year for admission into the Army, Navy, and Air Force wings of the NDA and the Indian Naval Academy.

📅 Important Dates (Expected)

Event Date
NDA I Notification December 2025
Last Date to Apply (NDA I) January 2026
NDA I Exam Date April 2026
NDA II Notification May 2026
Last Date to Apply (NDA II) June 2026
NDA II Exam Date September 2026

💰 Application Fee

Category Fee
General / OBC ₹100
SC / ST / Female Exempted

🎂 Age Limit

Candidates must be unmarried and born between the dates specified in the official notification. Generally, applicants should be between 16.5 to 19.5 years.

🎓 Educational Qualification

  • Army Wing: 12th Pass from a recognized board.
  • Air Force & Naval Wings: 12th Pass with Physics and Mathematics.

📊 Vacancy Details

The exact number of vacancies will be announced in the official notification for NDA I and NDA II separately.

📝 Selection Process

  • Written Examination (Mathematics & General Ability Test)
  • SSB Interview
  • Medical Examination
  • Final Merit List

💼 Salary & Benefits

During training, candidates receive a stipend of approximately ₹56,100 per month. After commissioning, salary and allowances increase as per defence pay scales.
