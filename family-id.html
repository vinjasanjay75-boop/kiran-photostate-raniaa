Parivar Pehchan Patra (Family ID) Haryana Details

Haryana Sarkar dwara chalayi gayi Parivar Pehchan Patra (PPP) yojna ke tahat har parivar ko ek vishisht ID di jati hai. Iske mukhya tathya niche diye gaye hain:

Zaroori suchna: Ab budhapa pension aur ration card seedha Family ID se bante hain. Agar aapki income ya date of birth galat hai, toh turant Kiran Photostate Rania par sampark karein.

Family ID ke Mukhya Labh:

  • BPL aur AAY Ration Card ki suvidha.
  • Ayushman Bharat / Chirayu Card ka labh.
  • Sarkari naukri mein extra marks ka labh.
  • Sarkari scholarship aur pension ka seedha labh.
