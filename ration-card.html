Haryana Ration Card Download 2026: Overview

Haryana sarkar ne Ration Card download karne ki prakriya ko ab poori tarah digital kar diya hai. Ab aapko kisi daftar ke chakkar katne ki zaroori nahi hai. Aap apne Family ID (PPP) se ghar baithe naya Ration Card nikal sakte hain.

Yad Rakhein: Ration Card download karne ke liye aapki Family ID mein mobile number link hona anivarya hai, kyunki verification ke liye OTP usi number par aayega.

Family ID se Ration Card Download Kaise Karein? (Step-by-Step)

1
Sabse pehle Food & Supplies Dept. ki official website epds.haryanafood.gov.in par jayein.
2
'Citizen Corner' menu par click karein aur 'Search Ration Card' ka option chunein.
3
Ab apna Family ID Number enter karein aur 'Get Member Details' par click karein.
4
Member select karein aur 'Send OTP' par click karein. Aapke mobile par 6 anko ka OTP aayega.
5
OTP verify hone ke baad aapka Ration Card screen par dikhai dega. Yahan se aap iska PDF Download kar sakte hain.
