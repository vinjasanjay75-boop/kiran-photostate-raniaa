📢 Recruitment Overview

The Indian Army invites applications for the Agniveer scheme, offering young candidates an opportunity to serve the nation for a period of four years. Recruitment is conducted for various categories such as Agniveer General Duty, Technical, Clerk/Store Keeper, and Tradesman.

🎓 Educational Qualification

  • Agniveer (General Duty): 10th Pass with minimum 45% marks and 33% in each subject.
  • Agniveer (Technical): 12th Pass with Physics, Chemistry, Maths, and English.
  • Agniveer (Clerk/Store Keeper): 12th Pass with minimum 60% marks and English/Maths/Accounts/Book Keeping.
  • Agniveer (Tradesman): 8th or 10th Pass (depending on the post).
