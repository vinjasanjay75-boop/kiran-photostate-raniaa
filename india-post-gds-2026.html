📢 Recruitment Overview

India Post is expected to release the notification for the recruitment of Gramin Dak Sevak (GDS) including Branch Postmaster (BPM), Assistant Branch Postmaster (ABPM), and Dak Sevak posts. Selection is based on merit prepared from 10th class marks.

🎓 Educational Qualification

  • Passed 10th (Matriculation) from a recognized board.
  • Knowledge of the local language of the concerned postal circle.
  • Basic computer knowledge.
  • Cycling knowledge is desirable.

📊 Vacancy Details

The exact number of vacancies will be announced in the official notification. Vacancies are released state-wise across India.

📝 Selection Process

  • Selection is based on a merit list prepared from 10th class marks.
  • No written examination or interview.
