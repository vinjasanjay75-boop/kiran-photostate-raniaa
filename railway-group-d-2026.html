Railway Group D Recruitment 2026

📢 Recruitment Overview

The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) is expected to release the notification for Group D (Level-1) posts. These include Track Maintainer, Helper, Assistant Pointsman, and other technical positions in Indian Railways.

📅 Important Dates (Expected)

Event Date
Notification Release July 2026
Application Start Date July 2026
Last Date to Apply August 2026
Exam Date November–December 2026

💰 Application Fee

Category Fee
General / OBC ₹500 (₹400 refundable after exam)
SC / ST / Female / PwD ₹250 (Refundable)

🎂 Age Limit

Criteria Details
Minimum Age 18 Years
Maximum Age 33 Years
Age Relaxation As per Government Rules

🎓 Educational Qualification

Candidates must have passed 10th (Matriculation) from a recognized board. ITI or National Apprenticeship Certificate (NAC) may be required for certain posts.

📝 Selection Process

  • Computer Based Test (CBT)
  • Physical Efficiency Test (PET)
  • Document Verification
  • Medical Examination

💼 Salary Details

Selected candidates will receive a salary under Pay Level-1 of the 7th Pay Commission, with an initial basic pay of approximately ₹18,000 per month plus allowances.
